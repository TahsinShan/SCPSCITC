Robotics & Automation

Our Robotics Sector is where ideas turn into real, working machines. Students explore electronics, sensors, coding, and engineering concepts to design, build, and program robots that solve real-world problems.

  • Electronics & Hardware Basics: Arduino, ESP32, sensors, circuits, and motor control.
  • Hands-On Robot Building: Line-followers, obstacle avoiders, smart traffic systems, IoT projects, and automation prototypes.
  • Programming for Robots: C/C++, MicroPython, sensor integration, and movement logic.
  • Engineering Thinking: Schematics, debugging, optimization, testing, and modular design.
  • Showcase & Competitions: In-house robotics expos, inter-school competitions, and national-level innovation challenges.

Graphics Design & Creative Media

Our Graphics & Creative Media Sector empowers students to express ideas visually, master modern design tools, and create impactful digital content. From branding to illustrations, members learn to turn creativity into skill.

  • Portfolio Development: Students build personal design portfolios to showcase skills and growth.
